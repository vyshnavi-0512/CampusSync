🚀 Unique Features to Add
1. AI Teammate Finder
Not just "browse profiles" — input your project idea and the AI recommends the top  students whose skills, availability, and interests complement yours. 
2. Skill Gap Detector
When you create a project or group, the platform automatically scans your current team and highlights what skills are missing — then suggests specific students who fill those gaps in real time.
3. Campus Innovation Score
Each student gets a dynamic rating  based on projects completed, events attended, skills added, and collaborations formed. This gamifies engagement without being superficial — it becomes a portfolio signal.
4. Project Lifecycle Tracker
Every group/project goes through stages: Ideation → Building → Testing → Presenting. The platform tracks progress publicly, building credibility and helping judges or faculty follow real work happening on campus.
5. Open Bounties Board
Students or departments post micro-challenges with optional rewards (recognition, credits, certificates). Others can solo-solve or form teams to tackle them — driving organic collaboration around real problems.
6. Anonymous Idea Vault
Students can post ideas anonymously to test interest before committing. Others upvote, comment, or express interest in co-building. High-interest ideas surface to the top and attract collaborators naturally.
7. Smart Event Recommendations
Based on a student's skill profile and project stage, the platform recommends relevant upcoming events — not a generic list, but curated nudges like "You're building a fintech app — this workshop on financial APIs is happening Friday."
8. Collaboration Heatmap (Admin/Faculty View)
A visual dashboard showing which departments are collaborating, which skills are in demand, and where project activity is clustered — giving institutions real intelligence on campus innovation health.
9. Exit & Impact Log
When a project wraps up, the team logs what they built, what impact it had, and what they learned. This creates a public "Alumni of Projects" trail — a campus innovation portfolio that outlives graduation.
